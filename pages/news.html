Let's try Facebook!

Daniel Bouchard - August 10th, 2020

The Association of Computer Science Ethics

Computer Science ethics is a fairly niche interest. I'm looking to start a Facebook group that will let people with an interest in the topic discuss with others who also share the interest. I've put one post up so far- hopefully there's more to come! Please have a look and join if you're interested: The Association of Computer Science Ethics

Within the spectrum of Computer Science Ethics are many dilemmas such as AI and how to use it, privacy and security in an online age, and reliability of critical systems just to name a few. These things can capture the imagination of many; just pick up almost any science fiction novel and you can find a plethora of fascinating ways ethics are adressed in technology. By extension, as a software developer or someone in the field of technology, those issues are also our issues and we should probably think about what we're making.
